Void Whispers

Published on May 28, 2025

  • The void whispers screams in the silence,
  • Only for me to feel, to drown in its call,
  • I reach for the light beyond the abyss,
  • Yet my hands fall short, never reaching far.

Fleeting Away

Published on May 3, 2025

  • Life fleeting away, days count bad,
  • Like sand passing through my hand.
  • I cannot catch even a single grain,
  • Let alone catch up to life and not feel pain.
  • The sun still shines, but not for me, too far,
  • A dying light I see, behind a prison bar.
  • Each breath I take feels borrowed, not mine,
  • A ticking clock with no clear sign.
  • Dreams fade like ghosts down a hollow hall,
  • Each hope I held now condemned to fall.
  • Smiles are masks I barely wear,
  • Joy, my friend, it was never there.

Cannot Blame

Published on February 7, 2025

  • During night and day, I can’t forget your name,
  • Through roads, long and wide, memories haunt me.
  • I play songs, recite words, maybe then my soul will be freed,
  • Despite that, it is still chained by what’s been forged before.
  • Through future and past, regret now speaks my name,
  • Each step I take, a weight I cannot blame.
  • No wish remains to kindle former flame,
  • Just burdens wrought by words once left.
  • No matter what I do and do, I still lose you.
  • Didn’t I give you the sky’s stars and my heart?
  • Yet now each step I take is without you.
  • They lead me further and further, worlds apart.

Finite Sum

Published on January 8, 2025

  • The number of hours we have,
  • is a finite sum.
  • Each second subtracts a little more,
  • yet I want to steal infinity,
  • from a universe that won’t allow it.
  • Please, linger near the door,
  • hesitating on the edge.
  • Fill the air with unsaid words,
  • to keep me tethered here.
  • Please, don’t just leave.
  • Leave something—
  • a trace, a breath,
  • a memory that I can hold
  • in this fragile reality of ours.

Remember the Blue Nights

Published on December 28, 2024

  • Even the reddest of roses, wilts
  • During winter’s cold nights, it dies.
  • In the middle of the breeze, my hands freeze,
  • With nothing for me to hold onto.

To Do My Best

Published on December 13, 2024

  • I’ve tried to grow, to do my best,
  • Yet still, I fall and fail the test.
  • For what’s a life, no matter how free,
  • If I’m unseen by the one who I see?

Abyss of Despair

Published on November 15, 2024

  • Rest has not been bestowed upon my soul.
  • I suffer in silence and crumble inward.
  • Meaning useless in the face of the absurd.
  • Thousand fights and still a lot to build.
  • Not only my heart aches, but also my body,
  • Broken into pieces, shedding tears bloody.
  • I long for peace and serenity, love and tranquility,
  • Through shattered dreams and endless nights.
  • No cause will salvage myself,
  • As engraved on this soul’s shelf,
  • Wounds run deep beyond belief.
  • Screaming in the abyss of despair.
